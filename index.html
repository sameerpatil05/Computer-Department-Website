PVGCOE Nashik Computer Department

Pune Vidyarthi Griha's College of Engineering, Nashik (PVGCOE, Nashik) is an engineering college affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU). It is part of the Pune Vidyarthi Griha (PVG) group of institutions, which has a long-standing reputation for providing quality education in Maharashtra. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, including Computer Engineering.

Second Year (S.E)

The Second Year Computer Engineering program at Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) is designed to build a strong foundation in core computer science concepts, programming, and practical skills. The curriculum typically spans two semesters (Semester 3 and Semester 4) and includes a mix of theoretical subjects, laboratory work, and projects

Third Year (T.E)

The Third Year Computer Engineering program at Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) builds on the foundation laid in the first two years and introduces advanced topics in computer science, software development, and emerging technologies. The curriculum is divided into Semester 5 and Semester 6, with a focus on both theoretical knowledge and practical application.

Fourth Year (B.E)

The Fourth Year Computer Engineering program at Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) is the final stage of the undergraduate degree, focusing on advanced topics, specialization, and practical application through projects and internships. The curriculum is divided into Semester 7 and Semester 8, with an emphasis on preparing students for industry roles or higher studies.
